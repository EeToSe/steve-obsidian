# The Sleep King Protocol

**Objective**: Elite Recovery & Cognitive Performance
### Phase I: The Launchpad (Morning)

- **Morning Light (Circadian Anchor):** View sky outdoors facing East (10 mins).     
- **Physical Activation:** Movement early in the day to build sleep pressure (No HIIT 3h before sleep).
- **The Caffeine Rule:** No caffeine **10–12 hours** before target bedtime.
### Phase II: The Descent (Late Afternoon / Early Evening)

- **Sunset Calibration:** View the sunset outside (5 mins) to buffer evening light sensitivity.
- **Inverted Hydration Triangle**: Morning/Afternoon: Front-load. **2h before bed: STOP (Sips only)**.
- **Fueling Strategy:** Finish dinner **3-4 hours** & Glycine (3-5g) **1h** before bed.

- **Shield Up:** Blue-light blocking glasses **ON** & Use f.lux to warm temp and reduce brightness.

### Phase III: The Wind-Down (1–2 Hours Before Bed)

- **Digital Sunset:** Ideally all screens **OFF**.

- **Thermal Trigger:** ~10 min warm shower at **42°C** 1h before sleep (creates rapid cooling effect).
    
- **Analog Input:** Fiction reading, journaling, or non-work audio. No doom-scrolling.
    
### Phase IV: The Bedroom Environment

- **Temperature:** Ambient room temp **18–20°C** (Socks + Eight Sleep / Light bedding.).
    
- **Air Quality (Vital):** Crack a window or keep door ajar to manage CO2 levels.
    
- **Darkness:** Blackout curtains. Eye mask if necessary.

### Phase V: Touchdown (In Bed)

- **Timing Consistency:** Bedtime window: **22:00–22:30**. Wake time: Within **±15 min** daily.
- **Nervous System Override:** Double inhale nose, long exhale mouth x 3 reps.

### Phase VI: Optimization (Feedback Loop)

- **Check Wearable Data:** Monitor trends (Deep/REM), not daily scores.

- **The "Human" Check:** If data says bad but you feel good, trust your feeling.
